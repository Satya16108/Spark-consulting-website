Common questions

Before you reach out.

If you don't find your answer here, just ask us directly — we'll always give you a straight, honest answer.

How quickly can SPARK deliver first results?

Most clients see their first actionable insights within 30 days of engagement start. Our phased deployment model is designed for early wins — typically in procurement, inventory or production planning — that build confidence and pay for the next phase of work.

Do we need an IT team to implement Anviksha?

No. SPARK's implementation team handles all data integration, model setup and dashboard configuration. We work with your existing ERP, accounting system and data files — including Excel sheets, Tally exports and CSV data. Your IT team's involvement is minimal, typically limited to providing data access credentials.

What does a typical SPARK engagement cost?

Engagements are structured in phases to match SME budgets. A typical discovery and pilot phase ranges from ₹3–8 lakhs depending on data complexity and scope, with subsequent phases tied to delivered value. We share a detailed, transparent cost breakdown during the free assessment — no surprises.

Is our business data secure with SPARK?

All data is processed and stored within Indian jurisdiction. Anviksha uses enterprise-grade encryption, role-based access controls and full audit trails. We sign mutual NDAs before any data sharing and our infrastructure is aligned with SOC 2 security standards. Your data is never used for any purpose other than your own analytics.

What happens if our data quality is poor?

Poor data quality is the norm for most Indian SMEs — and we've built Anviksha to handle it. Our data profiling step identifies gaps and inconsistencies upfront. We then work with you to implement lightweight data quality controls as part of the engagement, improving data reliability progressively alongside analytics delivery.

Can SPARK work with our existing ERP or accounting system?

Yes. Anviksha has pre-built connectors for Tally, SAP Business One, Zoho Books, Microsoft Dynamics, Oracle NetSuite and many others. For less common systems, we use API integration or structured data exports. We've worked with virtually every ERP used by Indian SMEs and have never encountered a system we couldn't connect to.
