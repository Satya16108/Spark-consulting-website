How it works

From raw data to board-ready decisions in four steps.

STEP 01

Connect your data

Anviksha pulls from ERP, POS, shop floor, CRM and logistics via 60+ pre-built connectors. Tally, SAP, Zoho, custom APIs — all supported with minimal IT effort.

STEP 02

Build the intelligence layer

Our analysts configure sector-specific models, KPI hierarchies and data quality rules. No black boxes — every model is reviewed, explained and approved with your team.

STEP 03

Surface insights & alerts

Role-based dashboards surface the three decisions that matter most today. Live alerts, variance explanations and weekly narrative summaries pushed to your inbox.

STEP 04

Act and measure outcomes

Guided action workflows take the team from insight to execution. Outcome tracking closes the loop — you see exactly what the analytics delivered in rupee terms.
